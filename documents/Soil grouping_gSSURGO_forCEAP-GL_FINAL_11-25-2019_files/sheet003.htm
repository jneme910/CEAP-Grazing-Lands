APEXgraze SSURGO table name SSURGO physical name NCSS APEXgraze definition (rows 11-25) and SSURGO data definition (rows 26-47)
Z chorizon hzdepb_r hzn_bot    Depth from the soil surface to the bottom of the layer (m) (Range: 0.01-10)
BD chorizon dbthirdbar_r db_13b    Moist Bulk Density (Range: 0.5-2.5)(t/m3) ρb = MS/VT.Bulk density values should fall between 1.1 and 1.9 Mg/m3.
UW chorizon wfifteenbar_r w15l2    Soil water content at wilting point (Range: 0.01-.5) Soil water content at 1500 KPa or -15 bars (m/m) (0 if unknown).
FC chorizon wthirdbar_r w3cld    Soil Water Content at field capacity (Range: 0.1-0.6) Soil water content at 33 KPa or -1/3 bars (m/m) (0 if unknown).
SAN chorizon sandtotal_r sand_tot_psa    Sand Content (Range: 1-99) The percentage of soil particles which have a diameter between 2.0 and 0.05 mm.
SIL chorizon silttotal_r silt_tot_psa    Silt Content (Range: 1-99) The percentage of soil particles which have an equivalent diameter between 0.05 and 0.002 mm.
PH chorizon ph1to1h2o_r ph_h2o    Soil pH. (Range: 3-9) The pH of a solution in equilibrium with soil. 
SMB chorizon sumbases_r base_sum    Sum of Bases (Range: 0-150) The sum of bases (Ca++, K+, etc.) on the cation exchange complex. (cmol/kg) (0 if unknown).
WOC chorizon om_r calc_OC    Organic carbon concentration (%).(Range: 0.1-10)
CAC chorizon caco3_r caco3    Calcium carbonate content of soil (%) (Range: 0-99) (Blank if unknown).
CEC chorizon cec7_r cec_nh4    Cation exchange capacity (Range: 0-150) (cmol/kg) (0 if unknown).
ROK chfrags fragvol_r wpg2    Coarse fragment content (Range: 0-99) The percent of the sample which has a particle diameter > 2 mm. (fraction) (0 if unknown).
BDD chorizon dbovendry_r db_od    Dry Bulk density (oven dry) (Range: 0-2.0) Density of the soil after oven drying (t/m3). (0 if unknown)
SATC chorizon ksat_r *    Saturated conductivity (Range: 0.00001-100) Rate at which water passes through the soil layer, when saturated. (mm/h). (0 if unknown)
ECND chorizon ec_r ec_satp    Electrical Conductivity (Range: 0-50) Conductivity of electricity through water or an extract of soil (mmho/cm)

cogeomorphdesc geomfname * Step 2 for getting the Landform Kind:  This selects the landform Kind from only the components whose "geomftname" = "Landform".
